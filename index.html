Plant Care Guide

Everything you need to know to keep your plants thriving

Watering Basics Beginner

  • Check soil moisture before watering
  • Water early morning or evening
  • Use room temperature water
  • Ensure proper drainage

Light Requirements Beginner

  • Match light to plant needs
  • Rotate plants weekly
  • Watch for signs of light stress
  • Consider grow lights in winter

Temperature Control Intermediate

  • Maintain consistent temperatures
  • Avoid cold drafts

Maintenance Intermediate

  • Remove dead or yellowing leaves
  • Pinch growing tips to encourage bushiness

Common Plant Problems

Drooping Plants

Common Causes

  • Underwatering or overwatering
  • Sudden temperature changes
  • Poor drainage in pots

Solutions

  • Check soil moisture regularly
  • Use pots with drainage holes
  • Adjust watering based on season

Yellow Leaves

Common Causes

  • Overwatering
  • Nutrient deficiencies
  • Low light conditions

Solutions

  • Reduce watering frequency
  • Use balanced fertilizer
  • Move to a brighter location

Brown Leaf Tips

Common Causes

  • Low humidity
  • Salt build-up in soil
  • Underwatering

Solutions

  • Mist plants regularly
  • Flush soil monthly with water
  • Ensure consistent watering

Seasonal Care Calendar

Spring

  • Increase watering frequency
  • Start fertilizing
  • Repot if needed
  • Move plants to brighter spots

Summer

  • Water more frequently
  • Provide shade from intense sun
  • Increase humidity
  • Prune actively growing plants

Fall

  • Reduce watering
  • Stop fertilizing
  • Move plants indoors
  • Check for pests

Winter

  • Water sparingly
  • Provide extra humidity
  • Use grow lights if needed
  • Avoid cold drafts
